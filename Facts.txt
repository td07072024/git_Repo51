Chacha Chaudhary is an Indian comic book series, created by cartoonist Pran Kumar Sharma.[1] The comic comes in ten Indian languages including Hindi and English and has sold over ten million copies.[2] It has also been made into a 2002 live action television series, with Raghubir Yadav playing the role of Chacha Chaudhary, and a 2019 animated television series by Toonz Animation.[3]
